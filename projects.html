Deutche Telekom CO 2030

Aim : To reduce the energy cost of Core Central Offices
Procedure : During my Mastertheses i devised a optimisation model to reduce the Energy cost of Core central Offices, which are spread throughout Germany. The model finds the optimal value of PV and BESS installation to reduce the Energy cost, considering the installation cost of PV and Batteries
Outcome : By installing optimal value of PV and Batteries around 15% of energy const costs can be saved, even after considering for the installation cost of PV and Batteries.
Applied skills: python, Energy Market, Energy Modelling, SQL,
