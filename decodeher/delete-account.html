Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. To delete your DecodeHer account and associated data, follow the steps below.

Option 1 — In-App (Recommended)

  1. Open the DecodeHer app.
  2. Tap your profile icon or go to Settings.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion. Your account is removed immediately.

Option 2 — Email Request

If you can't access the app, email us at contact@pvfreund.com with the subject line "Delete My DecodeHer Account". Include the email address linked to your account. We will process your request within 7 days.

Delete Specific Data (Without Deleting Your Account)

You don't need to delete your account to remove specific data. All conversation data is stored locally on your device and is fully under your control within the app.

  • Delete a profile: On the home screen, long-press any profile card and confirm deletion. This removes the profile and all its messages, decoded results, and ask sessions.
  • Delete a message: Inside a profile, long-press any message to delete it individually.
  • Delete all local data: Uninstalling the app permanently removes all profiles, messages, and conversation history from your device.

None of this requires contacting us — it happens instantly within the app. Your account and subscription remain active.

What gets deleted

  • Your Firebase authentication account (email, Google profile link).
  • Your subscription tier record.

What is not on our servers

  • All conversation history, profiles, and decoded messages are stored only on your device. They are automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.
  • We have no server-side copy of your conversations.

Retention period

Account data is deleted within 7 days of a confirmed request. Subscription records may be retained by RevenueCat for up to 90 days as required for billing dispute resolution.
